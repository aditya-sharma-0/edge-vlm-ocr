Select a massive frontier model (e.g., Qwen2-VL-72B or LLaVA-OneVision) to act as the Teacher.

Feed the Teacher your formatted dataset and have it generate high-quality, pristine "Thinking with Visual Primitives" reasoning traces for every single degraded image.

Filter the Teacher's outputs, discarding any hallucinated coordinates or incorrect VQA answers to ensure the Student models only learn from perfect data.

Phase 4: Approach 1 - Direct Fine-Tuning (The Baseline)
Set up a LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation) fine-tuning script for SmolVLM-256M.

Train SmolVLM-256M directly on the formatted dataset without the Teacher's reasoning traces, forcing it to learn the bounding box generation entirely on its own.

Repeat the exact same direct fine-tuning process for SmolVLM-500M.

Save these baseline checkpoints.

Phase 5: Approach 2 - Knowledge Distillation (The SOTA Push)
Set up the Knowledge Distillation training loop, utilizing the Teacher's perfect spatial CoT traces.

Train SmolVLM-256M to mimic the Teacher, aligning its hidden states and logit distributions so it learns how to chain spatial thoughts together without logical collapse.

Repeat the distillation process for SmolVLM-500M.

Save these advanced SOTA checkpoints.

Phase 6: Micro-Testing & Hyperparameter Optimization
